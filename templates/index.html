Don't let your trauma stop you from slaying.

Triumph Over Trauma (TOT) was made to help individuals suffering from Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) manage their heart rate and oxygen saturation levels by recording and logging those factors in your own personal log.

Heart Rate and

Oxygen Saturation Logger

Our logger detects both your heart rate and oxygen saturation levels and logs them on your account. This information can be used to identify panic attacks and choose the best-fitting coping mechanism for you.

Coping Mechanisms

Explore our large selection of personally-made breathing exercise, self-talk, and mindfulness videos to cope with attacks here. Want to share your thoughts on the videos? Send us your story through our "Conact Us" page!
